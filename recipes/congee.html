Congee Description

Congee is a delicious dish! This dish has many different variations, and is called many different things by many different cultures!

The recipe shown here was taken from Made With Lau. You can find the original recipe here

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white rice
  • 12 oz chicken
  • 8 cup water
  • 0.5 oz ginger
  • 2 pieces green onion
  • cilantro to taste
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1.5 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Wash rice thoroughly to prepare it for cooking.
  2. Boil water and chop the vegetables. Chop the ginger until thing strips, and the cilantro and green onion into small pieces.
  3. Prepare the chicken. Cut it into thin slices, then marinate it with the oyster sauce, cornstarch, chicken bouillon (0.5 tsp) and water. Then add the vegetable oil to lock in the marinade.
  4. Start boiling the rice. Wait for the water alone to boil, then add the rice and immediately stir. Do not touch the rice again until it boils again. Then, turn the heat to medium and partially cover the pot. Allow to cook for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Break up the rice. Use a whisk to break up the rice more.
  6. Cook chicken. Put it in slowly into the pot and allow to cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add ginger, salt, and chicken bouillon. Garnish with Green onion and cilantro.
  8. Serve!
