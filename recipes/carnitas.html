Carnitas Description

Carnitas are a delicious addition to your arsenal as a home cook. Added to that is the use of an Instant Pot - combine the two and you have fall-off-the-bone, delicious, mouth-watering goodness!

Here's the location of the original recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4-5 pounds lean boneless pork roast, cut into 2-in chunks
  • fine sea salt and freshly-cracked black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • chopped cilantro, diced white or red onion, and/or fresh salsa
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly-cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix together chicken stock, garlic, orange juice, juice from the lime, ground cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Season pork chunks on all sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Use the Instant Pot! Click the Saute setting, add oil and the pork to sear it on all sides.
  4. Pour in the mojo sauce and toss briefly to combine.
  5. Close lid securely and set vent to "Sealing". Cook on high pressure for 30 minutes followed by a 15 minute natural release.
  6. Set oven to Broil.
  7. Remove pork and shred with two forks. Spoon juices on top of the pork and toss to combine. Broil for 4-5 minutes until the edges of the pork brown and crisp. Repeat until you are satisfied.
  8. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro, then serve with the salsa and onions and whatever you want!
