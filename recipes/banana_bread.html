Banana Bread Description

Banana bread is a must have recipe for leftover bananas that you don't know what to do with. This recipe works best with overripe bananas, since they're easier to mash up and lead to a more moist bread!

The recipe here is inspired by thisrecipe from All Recipes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 4-5 mashed overripe bananas
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • (optional) chocolate chips or cranberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F, and pregrease your pan with butter.
  2. Mix all ingredients together in this order: bananas, butter, eggs, brown sugar, vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, salt, optional ingredients
  3. Bake for 50-60 minutes. Check to see if it is done with a toothpick.
